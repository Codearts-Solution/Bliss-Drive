Google Search is still growing

There are more than 3.5 billion searches on Google every day. That's more that 40,000 searches per second.

And contrary to what you might have heard, Google is still a key growth driver for companies that know how to leverage it.

But the game has changed...

The 'new' SEO game

SEO is a very different game today than it was a few years ago. Here are just a couple of the things that have changed:

  • Mobile search has surpassed desktop search
  • Google AdWords takes up 4 positions on competitive keywords
  • Expanded AdWords ads are pushing organic search 'below the fold'
  • Google Maps & Local listings appear for most local searches
  • Ad blockers are pushing organic results higher
  • Position Zero getting an unfair amount of traffic from search

These are just a few of the updates. There are a lot more.
